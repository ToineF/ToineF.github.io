Game Jams - Team

A list of short projects I made with a team, often as the only programmer in my team.

  • Turbo Baby

  • Itch.io

Project : Become a baby wishing to join a family! Your goal is to make it as far as possible by dodging and breaking obstacles on the way. Help yourself with objects on the road and set off for a frantic race.
Context : 2D Endless Runner made for the Ludum Dare 53 in 48h in Game Maker with a team of 5 people.
My role : Only programmer of the team.




  • The Wizards' Hamlet

  • Itch.io

Project : Welcome to a strange hamlet where musicians were transformed into instruments. Use your reasoning skills, write down clues in your notebook, and find out whodunit, howdunit, and whydunit.
Context : Point 'n click made as a student project in 2 weeks in Unity with a team of 6 people.
My role : Only programmer of the team.




  • Let My Fish Alone

  • Itch.io

Project : Hungry seagulls are maliciously eating your fish! Take revenge by shooting them with your trusted dolphino-gun, an explosive pufferfish-launcher!
Context : 2D Plateformer made as a student project in 2 weeks in Unity with a team of 6 people.
My role : Only programmer of the team.




  • Slap King

  • Itch.io

Project : A depressed and unamused king with a fragile heart needs to lap the unfunny jesters in rhythm and let the servants bringing whooppe cushions pass to stay alive.
Context : Rhythm game made for the Global Game Jam 2024 in 48h in Unity with a team of 7 people.
My role : Only programmer of the team.




  • Shield Knight

  • Itch.io

Project : A princess takes on the army of goblins with sword-shield, a powerful weapon that allows its user to shoot back enemy projectiles to save her love.
Context : Top Down Shooter made as a student project in 1 month in Game Maker with a team of 6 people.
My role : Only programmer of the team.




  • Resolux

  • Itch.io

Project : Logical-gates based puzzle game about turning objects on.
Context : Minimalist puzzle game made as a student project in 3 days in Unity with a team of 6 people.
My role : Only programmer of the team and game designer.




  • Synthetic Recall

  • Itch.io

Project : Memory game that shows you halloween-themed dioramas you must successfully reproduce.
Context : Memory game made as a student project in 48h in Unity with a team of 6 people.
My role : Lead programmer of the team.




  • Fairway

  • Itch.io

Project : A golf game where you can play in different environments and use the wind and ice to guide you towards your destination.
Context : 3D Golf game made as a student project in 2 weeks in Unity with a team of 5 people.
My role : Only programmer of the team.
